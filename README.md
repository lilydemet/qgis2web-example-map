# qgis2web-example-map
Example qgis2web map hosted with Github Pages. 
Data from The Government of Canada Open Data ([2016 Provincial Boundaries](https://open.canada.ca/data/en/dataset/a883eb14-0c0e-45c4-b8c4-b54c4a819edb) and [Watercourses and Waterbodies](https://open.canada.ca/data/en/dataset/9d96e8c9-22fe-4ad2-b5e8-94a6991b744b), and from the Toronto Open Data Portal (City of Toronto [Regional Municipal Boundary](https://open.toronto.ca/dataset/regional-municipal-boundary/); [Green Spaces](https://open.toronto.ca/dataset/green-spaces/); [Public Art](https://open.toronto.ca/dataset/public-art/)). Data from The Government of Canada is licensced under the Open Government Licence - Canada; Data from the City of Toronto is licensed under the Open Government Licence – Toronto.
